About Me

I’m Angelo Meniconi — a freelance web designer, consultant, and multidisciplinary front-end developer. I help businesses, individuals, and brands around the world create professional, reliable websites and digital solutions that grow with them.

With over 10 years of experience, I’ve delivered projects ranging from small brochure sites and personal portfolios to enterprise-level eCommerce platforms and B2B systems. My work combines strategy, design, and development to ensure each project is not just functional, but purposeful.

My approach emphasizes clean, semantic code, strong performance, accessibility, and maintainability. I focus on building sites that are easy to use, easy to update, and designed to scale with your business.

As a one-man consultancy, you work directly with me from start to finish — no account managers, no handovers, no unnecessary layers. This ensures clear communication, agility, and a personal, client-focused experience.

I regularly work with small businesses, startups, creative professionals, and agencies needing trusted front-end development or consultancy support. Whether you’re launching a new project, improving an existing website, or looking for expert guidance, I provide clear, actionable solutions without the jargon.

How I Work

  • Freelance: direct work with individuals and businesses, providing end-to-end development and consultancy.
  • White-Label: trusted development support for agencies looking for reliable front-end expertise.
  • Contract: short or long-term consultancy and front-end development to support digital projects.

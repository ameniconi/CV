# Cookie Policy

This website ([angelomeniconi.co.uk](https://angelomeniconi.co.uk/)) uses cookies to improve your experience and track usage via Google Analytics. This policy explains what cookies we use, why, and your choices.

## What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember preferences and provide insights about how visitors use the site.

## Cookies we use

- ``_ga``, ``_gid``, ``_gat`` – Tracks site usage and events (Analytics / Non-essential, provided by Google)  
- ``Other first-party cookies`` – Remember site preferences (Functional / Essential, provided by angelomeniconi.co.uk)  

> Note: If you add more third-party services via Google Tag Manager, their cookies may also be set, but only after you consent.

## Why we use cookies

- To understand how visitors interact with the site and improve content/performance.  
- To measure event tracking for analytics purposes.

## Your choices

- Accept or reject non-essential cookies via the banner shown on first visit.  
- Delete cookies via your browser settings.  
- Blocking analytics may reduce accuracy of usage statistics.

For further information, see our [Privacy Policy](/privacy-policy/).

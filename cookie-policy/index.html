This website (angelomeniconi.co.uk) uses cookies to enhance your experience and track usage via Google Analytics. This policy explains what cookies are used, why, and your choices.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember preferences and provide insights about how visitors interact with the site.

Cookies We Use

  • _ga, _gid, _gat – Tracks site usage and events (Analytics / Non-essential, provided by Google)
  • Other first-party cookies – Remember site preferences (Functional / Essential, provided by angelomeniconi.co.uk)

Why We Use Cookies

Cookies are used to understand how visitors interact with the site, improve content and performance, and measure event tracking for analytics purposes.

Your Choices

  • Accept or reject non-essential cookies via the cookie banner on first visit.
  • Delete cookies via your browser settings at any time.
  • Blocking analytics may reduce the accuracy of usage statistics.
